ABOUT US

M. | Studio (pronounced m-dot-studio) was created in 2020
by Christopher A. Moody, an up and coming software engineer,
who set out to provide a creatively stimulating place
where designers of bespoke keyboards could craft creations and showcase their works. A place
where they could cultivate their ideas into a diverseful, successful brand.

Chris set out to make a space that felt all inclusive, where each designer felt encouraged
to explore their limits and challenge the status quo of keyboard design. It needed
to be set up in a way that it provided each designer their own space to work on projects
but also encourage group efforts with several commune and meeting spaces.

With these goals in mind the main layout out of M. | Studio has several sectioned off
workspace, with open fourth walls, all equiped with everything a designer could need
as well as ample room for any essential additions.
Each section combines into a common area where creators can present projects and perhaps
plan collaborations on future projects.

All these sections combine into the spacious main exhit hall where each designer
is provided a well accommodating area to display their latest works. Each designer is
encouraged to individualize their displays with provided materials as well as
any outside material they would like to utilize.
We hold showcases every first Saturday of the month, with available time slots
for individual showcases(subject to availability, please contact administor for more details).

If M. | Studios speaks to you and you'd like to become part of the family
please fill out or contact form below. We look forward to your future with M. | Studio!
